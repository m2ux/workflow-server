## Open Assumptions

None. All design judgements are technical and resolved under delegated authority; the consolidated design is presented to the user for approval at the design gate (not as per-assumption interviews).

## Wrap-Up

9 assumptions — all validated, except DP-2 (corrected: contract keying refined from the eval report's id-keyed sketch to content-keyed). One takeaway: C2's primitives already exist (`delivery.ts` + content-keyed block precedent in get_activity); the work is extending them into the `get_technique`/eager-bundle composition path, keeping strict channel isolation and opt-in semantics. C12 rides the same ledger with a single whole-bundle key.
