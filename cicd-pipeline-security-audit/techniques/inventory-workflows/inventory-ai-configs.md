## Capability

Inventory the AI configuration files per target for P6 detection, recording the presence and paths of `CLAUDE.md`, `AGENTS.md`, `.cursorrules`, and `.cursor/rules/`.

## Protocol

### 1. Inventory AI Configs

- For each target, record the presence and paths of AI configuration files (`CLAUDE.md`, `AGENTS.md`, `.cursorrules`, `.cursor/rules/`) into `{ai_config_inventory}` for P6 detection.
