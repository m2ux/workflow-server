## Capability

Execute the approved investigation plan area by area within the probe budget and gather the results into one consolidated evidence base. The operations in this set decompose evidence gathering into the per-area scatter unit and the ordered combine — the sequential scatter-gather pair the area-probe loop drives.

## Inputs

### probe_budget_per_area

Maximum probes per investigation area — the bound the per-area unit enforces and the consolidation validates.
